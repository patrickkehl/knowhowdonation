Know-how donation white paper eternized on the Ethereum blockchain

Every page of the white paper “Know-How Transfer Paradigm Change“ has been uploaded onto the Ethereum blockchain.

The Know-how donation white paper eternized on the Ethereum blockchain

The white paper “Know-How Transfer Paradigm Change” will live forever on the blockchain. It can no longer be altered and can be discovered independently from this homepage. The ideas behind will remain available for present and future generations as long as one copy of the blockchain exists.

Each page can be retrieved by the indiviual transaction that was used to write it onto the Ethereum blockchain.
