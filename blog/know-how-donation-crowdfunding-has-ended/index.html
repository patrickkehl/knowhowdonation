Know-How Donation Crowdfunding Campaign ended today

The know-how donation crowdfunding ended today after being open for 200 days. Within this time frame the site did grow in publicity. However, the funding goal was not reached and thus the made donations will be refunded by the contract. It seems the time was not yet ripe to go further. The main goals of the campaign were reached, to ensure the concept is public and some initial traffic is generated on the site. The story of an idea has started and it remains exiting to follow if, when and how this leads to benefits for society at large…
