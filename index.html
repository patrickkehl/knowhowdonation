Fundraising for a new Know-How Donation Foundation

                  

A know-how transfer paradigm shift can change the future of humanity. There is a need for a new foundation and audit organization to incite such a know-how transfer paradigm change. This foundation will develop a know-how transfer standard that implements automatically timed know-how donations when 20 years have passed. The paradigm change will stimulate know-how generation and increase in the long run wealth for everyone as efficiency in operation increases the output of every endeavor.

The application of the standard will accelerate know-how generation by the better availability of know-how from time-proven real-life manufacturing and service processes. A time lag between the invention and general publication is required to keep the currently existing incentives on the inventors’ side alive.

The new standard will make the donation of know-how clearly defined and visible to the public. The developed standard will be a means on the foundation's mission to foster a cultural change in how know-how is generated, managed and distributed.                 

                            

Teaser statements

  
Know-how has by nature a timer on its usability for inventors.
  
When know-how is no longer actively applied, then essential parts are lost over time.
  
A timed know-how donation minimizes potential negative impact on business operations while making the giving transparent for society already today.
  
The concept of timed know-how donations is new and needs to be spread. There is a lack in infrastructure, mechanisms and rules that could currently promote timed know-how donations.
